SUPPORT PROVIDED BY HAPE PROJECT

- 05 May 2023: participation of 2 cybersecurity experts, DISICON conference, in Pristina

- 25 May 2023: HAPE Kick-off event

Event Meeting

HAPE project launching event occurred on 25th of May 2023, at the Europe House, in Pristina. The event was opened by the Head of Cooperation of the European Union Office in Kosovo (EUOK), Mr. Johannes MADSEN and speeches were delivered by the Minister of Internal Affairs, Mr. Xhelal SVEÇLA, the Ambassador of Italy, His Excellency Antonello de RIO and the Ambassador of France, His Excellency Olivier GUEROT. A short presentation about the project was delivered by the Team Leader to the attendees. The Ministry of Interior (MoI) of Italy was represented by the Head of International Police Cooperation Service, Br. Gen Guardia di Finanza, Giampiero IANNI and the MoI of France by the Diplomatic Adviser of the French MoI, Elie CAVIGNEAUX.

- 8 June 2023: Donors round table

HAPE project organized a donor round table on the 8th of June 2023, with other key donors in Kosovo. The aim was to avoid overlapping and redundancy in action plans and mutual matters of interest, in the field of organized crime, cyber security/cyber-crime, critical infrastructures and border & migration. In this meeting, all donors presented implemented activities, ongoing and planned. Key donors were invited to HAPE project's first steering committee, as observers, and the project's draft work plan has been widely circulated to the key international projects that provide similar assistance to beneficiary institutions.

- 19 July 2023: 1st steering committee

Event Meeting

HAPE project organized a steering committee (SC) on the 19th of July 2023. This technical committee presented to the main beneficiaries of the project, MIA, Kosovo Police (KP), Special Prosecution of the Republic of Kosovo (SPRK), Police Inspectorate of Kosovo (PIK), Kosovo Academy of Public Safety (KAPS) and Kosovo Forensic Agency (KFA), the action plan for the next 3 years, in strategic and operational support. According to multidisciplinary and cross-functional approach in many actions of the project, the support will also involve the Ministry of Justice (MJ), the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU), Kosovo Customs, the Tax Administration of Kosovo, and other relevant institutions. Regular SC will be held every four months to verify the achievements of the outputs as well as mandatory results and discuss future actions to be undertaken in the following four months.

- 21 – 23 November 2023: Workshop in PRIZREN on the national strategy on drugs

Event Meeting

The final workshop for the drafting of the national strategy on drugs took place in PRIZREN, from 21st to 23rd of November 2023. The workshop brought together 43 participants from various institutions, with a notable presence of beneficiaries of the HAPE project. The initial day was devoted to finalizing contributions for the strategic content, while the subsequent day centered on formulating the action plan. The concluding day was specifically dedicated to addressing indicators and activities aligned with the overarching workplan.
