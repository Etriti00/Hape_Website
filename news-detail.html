SUPPORT PROVIDED BY HAPE PROJECT IN 2023

- 14 December 2023: HAPE Project Second Steering Committee Meeting

Event Meeting

The Home Affairs Programme of the European Union (HAPE) Project held its second Steering Committee Meeting on 14 December 2023 in Pristina. The meeting was co-chaired by Mr. Massimo Prozzo - Task Manager of the European Union Office in Kosovo and Mr. Ron Krasniqi - Acting Head of Division for Policy Coordination - Department for European Integration, International Cooperation and Policy Coordination of Ministry of Internal Affairs.

- 21 – 23 November 2023: Workshop in PRIZREN on the national strategy on drugs

- 25 October 2023: planning of the annual training plan in 2024, for the new certifications in the KFA

- 24 October 2023: recruitment of a local expert for the drafting of the national strategy on drugs

- 12 October 2023: team of two Spanish investigators in mission in PRISTINA

- 26 September 2023: two KP officers in the European Chiefs of Police Convention in the HAGUE

- 20 September 2023: start of the MIA workshops on the national strategy on drugs

- 19 September 2023: one EU expert, assessment on compliance of the KP law with EU acquis

- 11 September 2023: training on Joint Investigation Team (JIT) in Germany for 4 special prosecutors

- 24 July 2023: two EU DG HOME experts for the Kosovo law on foreigners

- 20 July 2023: first HAPE Work plan for the next 4 months

- 19 July 2023: 1st steering committee

Event Meeting

HAPE project organized a steering committee (SC) on the 19th of July 2023. This technical committee presented to the main beneficiaries of the project, MIA, Kosovo Police (KP), Special Prosecution of the Republic of Kosovo (SPRK), Police Inspectorate of Kosovo (PIK), Kosovo Academy of Public Safety (KAPS) and Kosovo Forensic Agency (KFA), the action plan for the next 3 years, in strategic and operational support.

- 25 May 2023: HAPE Kick-off event

Event Meeting

HAPE project launching event occurred on 25th of May 2023, at the Europe House, in Pristina. The event was opened by the Head of Cooperation of the European Union Office in Kosovo (EUOK), Mr. Johannes MADSEN and speeches were delivered by the Minister of Internal Affairs, Mr. Xhelal SVEÇLA, the Ambassador of Italy, His Excellency Antonello de RIO and the Ambassador of France, His Excellency Olivier GUEROT.

- 05 May 2023: participation of 2 cybersecurity experts, DISICON conference, in Pristina
